What Davidson Academy Is

Davidson Academy is a free public school for profoundly gifted students. It offers a challenging academic experience designed for young people who score at or above the 99.9th percentile on approved intelligence or achievement tests. Admission is competitive and based on demonstrated advanced ability rather than age or grade level.

How the Curriculum Works

The curriculum is flexible and ability-based. Students progress not by age, but by demonstrated mastery. Courses cover advanced mathematics, sciences, humanities, and interdisciplinary projects designed to challenge and stimulate high-level thinkers.

Locations and Options

There are two main offerings: the on-campus school in Reno, Nevada, and the online Davidson Academy Online program. Both provide rigorous academic environments but differ in format and daily structure.

Why It's Not a Typical School

The Academy emphasizes deep inquiry, self-direction, and intellectual community. Students often take university-level courses, engage in advanced research, and develop skills needed for high-level academic and professional paths.
